Ontology Specification Draft

Abstract

he Football Ontology is used to model the world of football and external factors such as GDP and World Health Organization health statistics for each country in the world. This ontology was created as a part of a group project for the module CS7IS1: Knowledge and Data Engineering, taught in Trinity College Dublin by Professor Declan O’Sullivan. The football ontology models various aspects of football, including but not limited to players, clubs, competitions, player appearances in football games, competitions, football leagues and football transfer mechanics. The football ontology also models various factors regarding a country, such as its GDP and Health Statistics. The Health Statistics for each country modelled by this ontology include obesity rate among children, life expectancy, assistance to medical research, total alcohol consumption, total population and more.
